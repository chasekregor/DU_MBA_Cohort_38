

### Bonds:
- Bonds are used to finance projects
- State Rate/Coupon Rate/Coupon Rate: the rate that is hard-coded and stated in the bond contract; it does not change; it is used to calculate cash interest payments.
- Market: the interest rate investors expect to earn on this type of bon in the current market.
- Effective interest method: mandated by GAAP, essentially the same ammoritization of credit and principle balances on mortages
- At par or face: stated rate 7% = market rate &%
- premium: stated rate 7% > market rate 6%
- discount: stated rate 6% < market rate 7%
